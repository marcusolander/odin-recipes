Banana Milkshake Recipe

Description:

This Banana Milkshake recipe makes a thick and creamy shake with the perfect ratio of fruit to ice cream to milk. It's packed full of banana flavor and is pretty much irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 large banana
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • Whipped cream optional
  • Cinnamon sugar optional for topping

Steps:

  1. Combine ice cream, milk, banana, and vanilla in your blender, and blend until creamy and pourable.
  2. Pour into a glass, top with whipped cream and cinnamon sugar if desired, and enjoy.
